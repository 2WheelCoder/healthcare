Terms to Know

See Glossary

Premium

The amount paid monthly, quarterly or yearly for your health plan.

Deductible

Amount you must pay for health services before your insurer begins to pay for them. May not apply to all services.

Max Out of Pocket

Most you pay during a policy period before your plan begins to pay 100%. Never includes premiums, balance-billed charges, or health care not covered in your plan.

Network

Facilities, providers and suppliers you health insurer or plan has contracted to prove services. Depending on plan, services out of network may not be covered and may be more expensive.

Coinsurance

Your % share of the costs for a covered health care service. You pay coinsurance plus any deductibles you owe.

Plan Additions

A cap on benefits the insurance company will pay in a year while you are enrolled in that plan. These are sometimes placed on particular services such as prescriptions or hospitalizations. You must pay everything once a cap is reached.
